Considering the Southeast Asia shipping market as our core business, we have signed contract with the main carriers in this line, including Philippines, Malaysia, Indonesia, Thailand, Vietnam, Laos and Myanmar. In particular, we have great advantages in export shipping to Philippines and Vietnam, customs clearance and to door service in Malaysia, import shipping from Thailand, and FCL shipping to Myanmar and Laos. We provide the most competitive transportation scheme for our domestic customers and agents of Southeast Asia, ensuring the high-quality service.
