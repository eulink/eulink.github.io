In the 21 century, China embraces its third wave of immigration, including investment immigration, technology immigration and oversea study, which gives birth to an emerging service industry, immigration movement and transportation of personal luggage. Guangzhou Navigator has been targeting this emerging industry to develop a series of strategies by making use of oversea agent network and our own high-quality resources. In the meantime, under the principle of mutual benefit and win-win situation, we have long term and effective cooperation with many consulting companies in the field of oversea study and investment immigration. Clients from this rising industry are different from those who traditionally know very well the procedure of import and export, so we put ourselves in our customers’ shoes, being worried about what they are worried about, considering what they consider, providing every client with individualized, safe and reliable scheme for immigration movement and transportation of personal luggage.
