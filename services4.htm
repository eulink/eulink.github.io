Guangzhou Navigator set foot into the import market in 2011 and soon expanded our business into the fields of furniture, necessaries, mechanical component, food and feed and so on. Now we are the exclusive agent for the import business of Hongkong Royal Furniture Co,. Ltd. As a one-stop import service provider, with our global agent network and contract prices with major shipping companies, we provide great convenience for small and medium-sized enterprises who are in or entering this market by taking advantage of our own district(Nansha Free Trade Zone). In addition, we are not only familiar with all the procedure of import commodity inspection, but also having good relationship with the Customs and the Commodity Inspection Department, which help to lower the risk of being inspected.
